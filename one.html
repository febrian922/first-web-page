Maroon 5

Songs About Jane (2002):

Maroon 5 is a Grammy Award-winning American pop rock band from Los Angeles, California. Originally formed in the mid-1990s as Kara's Flowers, the group rebranded as Maroon 5 in 2001 and skyrocketed to fame with their debut album Songs About Jane. Known for their catchy hooks, soulful vocals, and genre-blending sound, the band has maintained lasting popularity with chart-topping hits and electrifying live performances. Their frontman, Adam Levine, also gained massive recognition as a coach on The Voice. Maroon 5's official website

The Members

  • Levine: lead vocals, rhythm guitar
  • Jesse Carmichael: keyboards, rhythm guitar
  • James Valentine: lead guitar
  • Matt Flynn: drums
  • PJ Morton: keyboards, backing vocals
  • Sam Farrar: multi-instrumentalist (touring/session member turned official)
